**3. Look at realistic range and charging time**

- Removable packs cited here typically deliver **60–80 miles (≈100–130 km)** of mixed riding on dual‑battery setups, with **3–6 hour charge times** from a household socket.[7][8][14][5]  
- Single smaller packs often give **40–60 miles** of mixed use, which may be enough for daily commuting.[7][13][14]  

**4. Confirm local availability and regulations**

- Some of these models are **Europe‑only**, **India‑only**, or limited to certain markets.  
- Check that the bike’s classification (moped vs motorcycle) matches your license and insurance requirements.
